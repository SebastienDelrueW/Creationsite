Meet My360 Mirror

What is My360 Mirror?

My 360 Mirror is designed to help you take care for the front and the back of your hairstyle, while your hands are
completely free.
Design is simple and easy, it consists of two mirrors, while one of them is installed on the wall and cannot be moved, the other is completely adjustable.

Functionality

The adjustable arm allows you to rotate the second mirror 360 degrees. This essentially allows you to see yourself from varies positions.
The movable mirror is two-sided and for your convenience, one of the sides is magnifying.
